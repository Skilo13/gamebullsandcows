Cows and Bulls Game

Instructions (English)

This is the game of Cows and Bulls. The computer generates a secret 4-digit code with unique digits, and your goal is to guess the code. For each guess, you will receive feedback in the form of Bulls and Cows:

  • A Bull indicates a correct digit in the correct position.
  • A Cow indicates a correct digit in the wrong position.

Use the input field to enter your 4-digit guess and click the Guess button. Good luck!
